Pressure Test Stand

In-progress deep-sea pressure test stand for validating underwater robotics hardware.

Highlights

  • Designed in SolidWorks and simulated in both SolidWorks and Ansys Mechanical.
  • Designed according to ASME Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code.
  • Intended to simulate 1000m depth (1500 psi) to test future underwater robotics platforms, accommodating up to 6-inch diameter Blue Robotics pressure-rated enclosures.
  • Uses a custom electric ram-driven piston cylinder to pressurize the system.
