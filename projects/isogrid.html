Isogrid Optimization

Finite element term project focused on optimizing an iso-grid chamber design.

Highlights

  • Simulation and sensitivity study conducted in Ansys Mechanical.
  • Study objective was to optimize titanium iso-grid chamber mass while maintaining a safety factor of 1.5.
  • Sensitivity study used response surface optimization with rib thickness and radial thickness as input parameters.
